A Brief History of the Internet

***Warning: all information presented on this page may be, and probably is, factually incorrect***

The internet is a fascinating, magical entity that allows us to learn from and communicate with other people from all over the world. While no one actually knows what powerful forces created the internet, historians have some ideas of how it originated.

Sometime prior to his ascendency to the Vice Presidency in 1992, Al Gore had a vision (possibly from God, but also possibly from alien entities) that prophecized a world in which all computers could be interconnected. In this world, every man and woman would have easy access to important "web sites" which would allow them to view news, special interests, chat with others, and [mostly] enjoy "adult content."

Today, Al Gore's vision has become a reality. The internet has expanded exponentially and information about any topic, no matter how weird or specific, can be found by a simple Google search. Click here to see for yourself.
